About Learn&Pass

Built for Nigerian students. Powered by dedication. Completely free.

Our Mission

Learn&Pass was founded on a simple belief: every Nigerian student deserves access to quality exam preparation resources — regardless of their financial background. We aggregate past questions, model answers, and educational news to level the playing field for secondary school and university-bound students across Nigeria.

What We Offer

  • ✓ Thousands of past exam questions across major Nigerian exams
  • ✓ Daily educational news curated for students
  • ✓ A clean, distraction-free study environment
  • ✓ Live support chat for every registered student
